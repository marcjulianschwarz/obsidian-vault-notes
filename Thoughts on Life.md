## Your Most Improbable Life

Pursue increasing _improbability_ in your life by becoming less predictable and more unique in order to create "exotropy" rather than sameness.

I think this is good advice to live a fun and exciting life that is worth talking about when you are old. You will have a lot to say and you will gain a huge amount of experience, knowledge and wisdom, just from the fact that you encountered so many situations that a predictable life would not get you to.

https://kevinkelly.substack.com/p/your-most-improbable-life

## Connecting with old friends

A practical tip to staying in touch with geographically dispersed friends is setting up recurring 30-minute 1:1 video calls (biweekly to bimonthly) with calendar invites, flexible rescheduling, and engaging conversation topics.
